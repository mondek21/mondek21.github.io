Tonty Canyon

By: Mike Ondek

Tonty Canyon is "temporarily closed" right now due to water eroding the trail. This closure has been long lasting and there is no immediate plan for the trail to be improved. Normally Tonty Canyon would be accessible from two different paths. The first access point is directly from Lasalle Canyon and the second access point is from the River Trail.
